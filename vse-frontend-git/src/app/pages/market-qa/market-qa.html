Stock Market Q&A

Everything you need to know to start your virtual trading journey.

What is VSE (Virtual Stock Exchange)?

VSE is a simulated trading platform that mimics real-world stock exchanges like the NSE. It allows you to practice trading without any financial risk by using virtual currency.

How does Virtual Trading work?

You are given a virtual bank balance upon signup. You can use this balance to buy and sell stocks at real-market prices. Your portfolio updates in real-time as market prices change.

What is the difference between NSE and VSE?

NSE (National Stock Exchange) is a real exchange where actual money is traded. VSE is a simulation modeled after the NSE for learning and practicing purposes.

Is any real money involved?

No. VSE is 100% risk-free. No real money is deposited or earned. It is purely for educational and simulation purposes.

Why should I use VSE before real trading?

Trading in the real market requires skill and emotional discipline. VSE helps you test your strategies, learn how market orders work, and understand market volatility without losing your savings.

How do I start trading today?

Simply click on the "Sign Up" button on the home page, create your account, and you will be instantly granted virtual credits to start building your portfolio!
